About Gie

Hey! Who's Gie?

If there is someone who is passionate about web design and development, graphic design, and web/browser games, it would be Reggie or Gie as fondly called by his friends.

His passion for the web dates back since the time immemorial and, comes with it, is his diverse skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, angular, react and some of content management system such as Wordpress and Drupal. He’s the tinker who can turn your PSD format website ideas or layouts into web masterpieces and what not.

But that’s not all. While he’s an avid gamer by heart, he’s a bookworm too. He crawls in from the console/computer games to board games to fiction and creative nonfiction books and the likes of which. To him, the sweet and familiar smell of books is home, coz why not?

Your web man isn’t all that he is. In a not-so-distant future, he sees himself as a Full-stack Javascript Developer and is currently learning deeper into some of the most popular Javascript technologies such as Angular and Node.js. He dreams of developing his own games

Now, you have a little bit of who’s Gie. Suite yourself and see down below for more...
